# Freeform Field (Optional)

The Freeform field is an optional fifth column for personal notes, context, or reflection.

This field has no prescribed format or content requirements.

## Common Uses

- Personal reflections on the collaboration
- Additional context not captured elsewhere
- Emotional or subjective aspects
- Language codes for non-English content
- Meaningful quotes or sentiments

## Examples

- "This insight changed how I think about architecture"
- "A joyful pairing session"
- "Loved mapping musical intent to visuals"
- "Your encouragement kept me going"
- Language indicator: "en", "es", "fr"
- "The family that moves the work together stays together"

## The Question It Answers

**"What else feels important to express?"**

The Freeform field is entirely optional and unrestricted.
