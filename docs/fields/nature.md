# Nature Field

The Nature field indicates the type or category of collaborator.

A short descriptor that clarifies what kind of entity contributed.

## Examples

- **Human**
- **AI** (or specific: "AI (Claude)", "AI (ChatGPT)", "AI (Gemini)")
- **Organization**
- **Poem** (with author if known: "Poem by Human")
- **Tool**
- **System**
- **Concept**
- **Book**
- **Film**
- **Song**

## Guidance

The Nature field is freeform and typically concise (1-3 words).

It answers: **"What is this collaborator?"**
